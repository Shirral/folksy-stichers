Our Embroidery Club!

Welcome to Folksy Stitchers!

Join us in a relaxed and informal atmosphere where threads come alive and creativity thrives. Together, we'll hone our skills, forge friendships, and nurture our mental well-being through the art of embroidery. Let's stitch together, one thread at a time, and create beautiful works of art while finding solace and joy in the rhythmic dance of the needle. Welcome to our vibrant community!

Our Meetings

Embroidery can be a great way to do something rewarding, meet new people, and even start a business!

We host meetings four times a week. Everyone is welcome - no matter if you're a beginner or a seasoned stitcher, you can pop by and start creating something beautiful right away!

Not sure where to start? Come to one of our themed meetings - the hosts will be prepared to show you the basic stitches and suggest beginner-friendly projects. You can also check out our tutorials for the stitches that we most commonly use so you come prepared!

Want to host a meeting?

If you have a brilliant concept for hosting a meeting or a special embroidery event, we invite you to reach out to us. Your creativity is what makes our club thrive, and we're always excited to explore new possibilities. Whether it's a workshop, a themed gathering, or a collaborative project, we're open to your suggestions. Let's come together, share our passion for embroidery, and create unforgettable moments. Contact us today - by email, or in person - and let's bring your idea to life!
